Overview

Purpose

The Dry Oar website will deliver an interactive experience by providing information regarding the many adventures we offer, allow clients to build a customized adventure and finally, allow them to book and pay for thier trip through the website.

Audience

Our target audience are in the age range of 8 years old and above. We cater to all levels of experience, from the very begginer to advanced adventurer.

About me

Growing Up

I grew up in a desert town outside of Los Angeles. Growing up I played a lot of sports such as Baseball, Football and Wrestling. I also really enjoy music. In my teenage years I played guitar and sang in a punk band. We were terrible but I didn't care, we were playing punk rock and it's suppose to sound terrible. Later in my 20s I got into DJing, and I was pretty good at it. I DJ'ed hundreds of events from the clubs of LA to weddings and corporate events. I even performed with some big-name artists. I also play the piano and make electronic music with my computer, synthesizers, and drum machines.

I've moved around all over California but when I met Justina, I knew she was the one and that it was time to put away the turntables and settle down. We got married and decided we wanted to move to Utah. I loved every place I've lived in and made a lot of friends, but I especially love living here in Utah. We are fans of all things outdoors and Utah is the place where we could spend as much time in the wild as we want. So many adventures to seek within minutes. Now we are a growing family with three kids and counting. Here is a picture of me with my wife Justina and three children Landon, Audrey, and our newly born baby Halen, like Van Halen.

So, the next chapter, getting into a new career. It was my interest in computer music that led me to discover a new passion, computer programming. I am still very much a beginner at it but due to my fascination with what I could do in music production, building out small scripts and devices, I want to be a programmer. With some self-taught skills in Web Development and SEO, I managed to get work with a cool company, Boostability, that focuses on SEO (Search Engine Optimization). Being self-taught I realize I have some gaps in my knowledge and have felt like I hit a wall in my growth. So, I decided to go back to school to learn more about programming. This website was built as a final project to my first class, and I created a page dedicated to my pursuit of what I have learned.

I am constantly looking for new things to learn about and experience. I have a love for life and a heart of gold. This website is just a 1.0 version. Like me, it will continually evolve and adapt to new and better versions. So please, come back occasionally, to see how myself and the website grows.

Oh, and just last year I took on a new hobby, photography. For being a beginner, I am impressed with myself. You can check out my photography page to see some of my pictures and the gear I use. Most of the pictures I take are landscape, Astro, and milky way photos. I am starting to build up my Instagram account so please follow me!

Photography

For the last 12 years while living in Utah, I have had an urge to learn about photography. Not just a point your phone and push the button kind of photography but I really wanted to learn more about how to take good pictures. I wanted to get one of those big camera bodies with all the buttons on it and learn what each one did. As an avid outdoors guy living in Utah, we have some of the best scenery in the world and I want to capture it.

So last summer I spent too much money and bought a Sony a7 III camera. After several hours of watching video tutorials and many days of practicing, I finally have some images I feel are worth sharing. I still have a lot to learn and hope to eventually get to a professional level.

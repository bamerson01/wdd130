Get to know me!

Hi! I'm Brady and that's my beautiful wife, Justina. Thank you for visiting my website. I built it for you to get to know me so it means a lot to me that you would stop by.

I grew up in a desert town outside of Los Angeles. Growing up I played a lot of sports such as Baseball, Football and Wrestling. I also really enjoy music. In my teenage years I played guitar and sang in a punk band. We were terrible but didn't care much, we were playing punk rock. Later in my 20s I got into DJing and I was pretty good at it too. I DJ'ed hundreds of events from the clubs of LA to weddings and corporate events. I even performed with some big name artists. I also play the piano and make electronic music with my computer, synthesizers, and drum machines.

I've moved around all over California but when I met Justina, I knew she was the one and it was time to put away the turntables and settle down. We got married and decided we wanted to move to Utah. Even though I loved every place that I lived in and made a lot of friends, I especially love living here in Utah. We are fans of all things outdoors so we wanted to live in a place where we could spend as much time in the wild as possible. Now we are a growing family with three kids and counting. Here is a picture of me with my wife Justina and three children Landon, Audrey and Halen.

So the next chapter, getting into a new career. It was my interest in computer music that led me to discover a new passion, computer programming. I am still very much a beginner at it but due to my fascination with what I could do in music production, building out small scripts and devices, I want to be a programmer. With some self-taught skills in Web Developement and SEO, I managed to get work with a really cool company, Boostability, that focuses on SEO (Search Engine Optimization). Being self-taught I realize I have some gaps in my knowledge and have felt like I hit a wall in my growth. So I decided to go back to school to learn more about programming. This website was actually built as a final project to my first class and I created a page dedicated to my pursuit of what I have learned.

Oh, and just last year I took on a new hobby, photography. For being a begginer I am actually impressed with myself. You can check out my photography page to see some of my pictures and the gear I use. Most of the pictures I take are landscape, astro and milky way photos. I am starting to build up my Instagram account so please follow me!

I am constantly looking for new things to learn about and experience. I have a love for life and a heart of gold. This website is just a 1.0 version. Like me, it will continually evolve and adapt to new and better version. So please, come back every once in while to see how myself and the website grows.

Web Developement

As I mentioned on the landing page, my hobby in computer music led me into a new passion, computer programming. How? Well, I like to use a program called Ableton Live to make and record music.

I have been using this program for 20 years. In the early years, it was not as feature robust but they provided access to a program called Max for Live, a graphical coding environment, sort of like a drag and drop code environment that allowed musicians to build devices to solve issues or create more efficient work flows when making music. I often found myself noodling around and building devices way more than I was actually making music. So here I am, enrolled at BYUi to pursue a degree in Web Development.

At the moment I am taking a course on web fundamentals and learning more about HTML and CSS. This website is my final project. I am also taking a course on Linux and learning how to navigate and create scripts in the shell command line. I have learned a lot so far and am looking forward to investing more of my time on this journey of learning. I will update this site with more info as I progress. I want to demonstrate and showcase my newly acquired skills. Here are just a few topics I know:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • PHP
  • Python
  • MySQL
  • WordPress
  • SEO (Search Engine Optimization

Photography

For the last 12 years while living in Utah, I have had an urge to learn about photography. Not just a point your phone and push the button kind of photography but I really wanted to learn more about how to take good pictures. I wanted to get one of those big camera bodies with all the buttons on it and learn what each one did. As an avid outdoors guy living in Utah, we have some of the best scenery in the world and I want to capture it.

So last summer I spent too much money and bought a Sony a7 III camera. After several hours of watching video tutorials and many days of practicing, I finally have some images I feel are worth sharing. I still have a lot to learn and hope to evenytually get to a professional level.
